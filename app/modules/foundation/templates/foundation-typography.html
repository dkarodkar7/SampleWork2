The fonts used in our typography were chosen based on the following rationale:

  • They support typography found elsewhere in our brand.
  • They perform well on web and mobile.

Headers & Body

Usage

For maximum readability:

  • Body copy should never run the width of the page.
  • Wrap text blocks — these perform well in responsive environments.
  • Type should never exceed 8 grid columns — 6 columns is preferable.
