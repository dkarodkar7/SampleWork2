Icon Strategy

Our family of icons and illustrations provides a core set that’s also extensible, which enables you to apply our iconography to deeper areas of your product as needed.

In general, we use illustrative icons in our product marketing — these reflect our brand’s broad concepts. For product functionality we use functional iconsÂ — these reflect actions, tasks, settings and other practical aspects of our products.

Need help with using our logo? Download logo guidance or logo files.

Illustrative Icons

Category illustrations

Our category illustrations reflect our five business units. They’re for use on welcome pages and in marketing materials. Don’t use them as icons or otherwise scale them down.

Brand Illustrations

Our brand illustrations represent the functions, benefits or qualities of our business units. They can be used for any welcome page, marketing materials or applications that tie to those units. As with our category illustrations, brand illustrations shouldn’t be scaled. Instead, use the brand icons shown below.

Brand Icons

Brand icons are simplified versions of the brand illustrations that can be used at smaller scales.

Functional Icons

Our functional icons are designed to convey a product’s commands, categories or common actions visually. Unlike our illustrations, they are “live’ objects in that they are used for navigation or button commands.
