Experience

We strive to design experiences that are:

Usable

The best products stay out of the user’s way, so they can complete tasks, find solutions and gain insights with a minimal outlay of time and effort. When it comes to choosing the right design or development asset for fulfilling a user’s need in the moment, you have lots of options. Usability relies on making the cleanest, most logical and most appropriate choice, every time.

Smart

Our products should reflect our knowledge of our customers. At the screen level this means:

  • Anticipating their questions and answering them
  • Using terminology that reflects their point of view
  • Providing important information when and where they’re apt to need it most
  • Using patterns and workflows that support how they’re likely to approach a task

Contemporary

Our customers expect us to keep up with technology trends and user interface standards — and with their needs. Staying competitive means presenting a modern face to the world through our digital products, ones that:

  • Use the latest available responsive web and mobile platform components
  • Speak to our customers in a voice that’s warm, conversational and human
  • Align with other Pitney Bowes properties, such as our web presence and offline communications products
