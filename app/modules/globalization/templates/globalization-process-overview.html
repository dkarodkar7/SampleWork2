All Pitney Bowes products should be developed with global considerations in mind, even if you’re only planning on a U.S. release now. Those plans could change, and how well you’ve planned ahead can make a big difference in how quickly, easily and affordably you can adapt your product for audiences outside the U.S.

Process

There is a set process at Pitney Bowes for globalizing your product. Fortunately, the Globalization Team’s deep experience and expertise can help you ease that process. Remember that it’s never too early to start preparing your product for global consumption.

About the Globalization Team

Our team is, not surprisingly, distributed around the world within the GUX group (engineering). Here are some quick facts about us:

  • We’re located in the U.S., France, Germany and Poland.
  • We consist of seven dedicated Project Managers working under one Globalization Director.
  • Our members’ native languages include Japanese, German, Swedish, Russian, Portuguese, Polish and French.
  • We’re trained in Agile and can serve as fully integrated members of your team as early as you wish.
  • You can reach us at globalization@pb.com or through our Yammer group.

Services We Provide

By Including globalization as a critical, early aspect of your development process you’ll ultimately be saving yourself time, headaches and money — it can mean the difference between fixing a bug once and having to fix it in every single localized product later on.

Here’s what we can provide:

  • Initial assessments of your product
  • Strategies for streamlining the process of making your product world-friendly
  • Early validation assessments using tools such as pseudo localization
  • Advice and expertise throughout design, development and testing
  • Complete management and oversight of all aspects of localization

Funding Considerations

We use professional translators and agencies, at competitive rates, but we don’t have a budget to buy those services ourselves. This means that, once Product Management has determined your product’s localization needs, you’ll need a Purchase Order to cover your localization costs. The Globalization Team can help you size your product’s requirements and provide an estimate. Be ready to provide your Cost Center when you contact us.
