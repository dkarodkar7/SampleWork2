Writing for Popovers and Modals

When writing copy for modals, popovers and tooltips, good writing principles apply. Try to keep your messages succinct, clear and to the point by:

  • Using active rather than passive writing
  • Focusing on what the user most needs to know
  • Constructing short sentences, written in plain language
