Writing

Style

  • Use short, active words and phrases.
  • Make sure your copy reflects our brand’s voice.
  • Don’t phrase your headers or sub-headers as questions.
  • Keep sentences short and paragraphs to 1-3 sentences maximum.

Capitalization and Punctuation

Top-Level Headers

  • Use title caps.
  • Don’t conclude with punctuation, such as periods or colons.

Second-Level Sub-Headers

  • Use title caps, unless they’re formed as a complete sentence, in which case they use sentence caps.
  • If formed as full sentences then conclude with a period.

Third-Level Sub-Headers

  • Use sentence caps.
  • Conclude with a period.
